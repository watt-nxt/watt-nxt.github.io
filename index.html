About

Learn More About Us

Piscataway-based FTC team Say Watt Robotics was founded by our coach, James Carr.

Some of our accomplishments this Center Stage season:

  • Ranked as the #1 team in our league
  • Set the NJ State Record on 1/20
  • won the Inspire Award at the League Meet on 1/20
  • won the Audacious Autonomous award at league meets twice

Ever since its debut in 2005 as a First Lego League team, Say Watt has grown by the numbers. As 7th-12th graders with a passion for STEM, we hope to work with those who interested in pursuing STEM-based careers to foster an interest in science, mathematics, engineering, and technology within our community and empower the engineers of tomorrow.

Outreach

We make a lot of Impact

Read about a few of our impactful outreach initiatives!

Mentoring a Team

Since December, a couple of our members have been personally mentoring the Brearly Bots (team 19227). We've helped them in both software and hardware, as well as general FIRST knowledge.

Summer Workshops

We host weekly workshops in the summer in Programming and CAD to educate the non-robotics community, as well as reinforce skills in existing team members.

Scrimmage Hosts

To foster gracious professionalism and coopertition in the NJ FTC community, we hosted a scrimmage at our facility. 5 teams attended!

Support Team at Tournament

At the Frozen Frenzy Tournament this year, we served as a support team. We helped the TCS Tigers host their tournament and coordinated some of the field/venue set up and logistics
